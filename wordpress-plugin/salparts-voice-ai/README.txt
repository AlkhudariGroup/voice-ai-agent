=== Sal Parts Voice AI ===

Voice AI assistant for Sal Parts - auto spare parts store in Germany.
Clients can tap the mic button and speak or chat to ask about products.

Installation:
1. Zip the salparts-voice-ai folder
2. WordPress Admin > Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin
3. Activate

Setup:
1. Go to Settings > Voice AI
2. Set your deployed Voice AI app URL
3. Customize store name and description
4. Save

The mic button will appear on your site. Click to open the full Voice AI experience.
