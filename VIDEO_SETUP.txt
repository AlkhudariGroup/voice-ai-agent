VIDEO AVATAR - How to use your video

1. Add your video file to: public/avatar-video.mp4
   - Or set NEXT_PUBLIC_AVATAR_VIDEO_URL in .env.local to your video URL

2. Video behavior:
   - PLAYS & LOOPS when: you talk (listening) OR agent speaks (TTS)
   - PAUSES when: both you and agent are silent

3. Video format: MP4 or WebM, short looping clip recommended
   - Muted (no audio) - required for autoplay in browsers

4. If video fails to load: falls back to "e" logo automatically
